Regarding the Offline Version

Following the end of service, the online version of KINGDOM HEARTS Union χ Dark Road will receive an update and become the offline version of the app.

※ An offline version will not be released for the Amazon App Store.
※ Any information that is inputted will only be saved to your device and it will not be sent to any outside party.
※ Uninstalling the app will result in all data being deleted.
※ In-game purchases will no longer be available.
