How to Play
■Battle






*If you lose in battle, you can use Charm to revive.
You may not be able to use Charm during certain parts of the quest.

■Main Quest/Main Mission
You can select main quest from the top right of the home screen to progress with the story.
When a main mission in the main story is available, you need to clear the main mission in order to move on to the next quest.

■World Battle
World Battle is a mode where you battle an endless number of enemies.
Make sure to use it to earn BP, filling up your album, and for accomplishing missions.
As you progress through the game, the difficulty rises.

* The maximum BP you can earn in one World Battle is 999,999,999.
* Daily reset occurs at 4:00AM and you will automatically be taken to the HOME screen.

■Cards
Cards can be drawn using Card Draw Tickets or Jewels.
You cannot own more than one of the same card, and obtaining the same card will automatically merge and increase its power level.
* There are cards with abilities that cannot level up.

The maximum level is +10 for the same card, and any of the same card beyond that can be traded for BP or SP (Shop Points).

■Leveling Up
Pressing Use BP on the STATUS screen will level up Xehanort.


■Automatic BP
BP automatically accumulates even outside of Battle and Missions.
Press the BP button on the top of the screen to accept the accumulated BP.
* The maximum amount of BP that can be accumulated is 50,000.


■Note
▼Force closing the app in the middle of play
・During Events: You will be returned to the first battle and any Charms that were used until that point will not be returned.
・During Main Quests: You will return to the HOME screen and any Charms that were used until that point will not be returned.
・During World Battle: You will return to the HOME screen, and you will be able to accept any accumulated BP but any Charms which were used will not be returned.

▼When updates occur in the middle of play
・During Events: Same as force closing the app.
・During World Battle: Same as force closing the app.
