About Draws

■ Draws
・Jewel Deals consume Jewels and Ticket Deals consume Card Draw Tickets. Please note that there’s a higher chance of getting powerful cards through the Ticket Deals.

Draw Type Single Card Draw 10-Card Draw
Jewel Deal 300 Jewels 3,000 Jewels
Ticket Deal 1 Card Draw Ticket 10-Card Draw Ticket

■ Cards
・Cards have 6 color-types in total: Light Red/Light Green/Light Blue/Dark Red/Dark Green/Dark Blue. You can equip up to 5 cards of every color-type (30 in total) in your card deck.

・You cannot own more than one of the same card, and obtaining the same card will automatically merge and increase its power.
* Certain cards do not increase its power upon being automatically merged. In this case, the PASSIVE EFFECTS in the Album will increase to be more than the standard amount.
　
・Each card can go up to +10 through the automatic merging of the same card. Any duplicates that are obtained after +10 can be converted to either BP or SP (Shop Points).

* You can choose whether to convert them to BP or SP through the settings. Also, the conversion rate of the cards differ depending on the card. 　
* Cards that do not have their power increased upon the automatic merging will have a slightly increased conversion rate after the 11th duplicate.
